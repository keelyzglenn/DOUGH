ABOUT US

We are a local Seattle pizza companythst strives for excellence in every pie.

We only use Pacific Northwest ingrediants to eleveate classic pizzas with a twist.

History

Our chef was born in Italy, where he learned how to cook a lot of really yummy stuff. Though he doesnt like to brag- he can cook up a freaking storm and aims to always make his customers gain 15 pounds per piece. We like to advertise our pizza as fresh, local, and very much centered in the Pacific northwest, but by that we just mean we only make our pizza out of what expired food we find in the dumbsters around Seattle. Which is why our slogan is "reduce, reuse, and never trust an expiration date"
